About

Jared Zimmerman is an award winning product design leader, educator, and speaker located in San Francisco, CA.

He's built products & lead teams at Google, Meta, Wikimedia Foundation, Autodesk, & Delivery Hero, and partnered with renowned agencies MetaDesign, Lunar, Punchcut, Iconmobile, Tellart and Ammunition.

For consulting, or speaking opportunities, please don't hesitate to reach out to him.
